The New Customer
Just joined. Zero to one order. Three possible paths in.

How they got here

  • Barbershop referral — "my barber uses this." Already pre-sold on quality.
  • Social discovery — IG/TikTok/YouTube tutorial. Curious, not committed.
  • Gift receiver — someone bought them a tin. Zero brand context, full wallet.

What they need from email

  • Confirmation they're in the right place — Schorem, Rotterdam, Leen & Bertus
  • A product map — 4 tiers (Wash → Prep → Style → Finish), not 30 SKUs
  • An incentive that matches the tone — free shipping over $35 beats a discount
  • An educational 2nd email — Schorem YouTube content, not "shop the sale"

The Returning Customer
Repeat purchase is the margin. Three sub-segments — three different emails.

Three sub-segments

  • Replenisher (~30% of list) — reorders same SKU every 60–90 days. Carries 50% of repeat revenue.
  • Collector / Upgrader (~20%) — bought one pomade, curious about the next. Highest AOV expansion.
  • Lapsed regular (~25%) — 6+ months quiet. Biggest single $ recovery lever.

What each needs

  • Replenisher: day-50 "your Pink is probably running low," 1-click restock
  • Collector: canonical pairings ("Grooming Tonic → Texture Powder") — education, not discount
  • Lapsed: lead with what's NEW since they left, not "we miss you 15% off"

THE ASK

Stop sending "we miss you 15% off"

Generic win-back gets ignored because every brand sends it. Lapsed Reuzel customers didn't leave because of price — they got their last tin at the barbershop or switched once on vacation. The hook is what's new, not what's cheaper.

Reframe the email
Lead
"You missed the Grooming Tonic Texture Cream launch"
Body
Top 3 new formulas since they left
Close
Free ship on reorder of their last SKU — framed as hospitality
BENCHMARK

Win-back · 3–6% reactivation

A strong win-back flow reactivates 3–6% of lapsed customers within 90 days. At Reuzel's list size (~six figures on the low end for 95-country barber awareness), recovering even 4% is meaningful repeat revenue.

Why it works for Reuzel specifically
1
Heritage product — scent/formula loyalty is strong once reminded
2
New launches cadence (Texture Foam, Texture Cream) = real news
